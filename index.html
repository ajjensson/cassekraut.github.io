cassekraut - a twist on "casse-croûte"

We intended to have fun with traditional sauerkraut which we kept in the traditional German spelling. cassekraut is a blend of English, Ukranian, Québécois, and German; which we think speaks volumes of what it means to be Canadian.

cassekraut places the utmost importance on sourcing the highest quality, seasonal, local and organic produce. We use fairtrade and wild-trade seeds and herbs as well.

We hope that you will taste the difference that the extra love we add gives to each and every product we offer.
